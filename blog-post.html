Why I'm building GroviaTech in public

I'm starting GroviaTech to help growing businesses fix the three things that quietly cost them the most: inconsistent social media, WhatsApp leads that go cold, and decisions made without real data. This blog is where I'll document that build, day by day — what I'm shipping, what breaks, and what I learn along the way.

Why write about it at all?

Two reasons. First, honestly — it forces clarity. If I can't explain a decision in a short post, I probably haven't thought it through enough. Second, the people this business is for (small and growing business owners) are exactly the people who'll find these notes useful. If a post about "the 5 numbers to track weekly" helps someone even before they're a client, that's a good outcome.

What you can expect here

  • Build logs — the tools, decisions, and mistakes behind GroviaTech's own systems.
  • WhatsApp CRM notes — practical breakdowns of automation flows that actually convert.
  • Dashboard & data posts — simple frameworks for tracking what matters, without needing a data team.
  • Social media playbooks — what's working (and what's noise) in automation right now.

The plan

Short posts, most days. Nothing that takes 20 minutes to read — if it can't be said in 300–500 words, it's probably two posts. Each one is written to be easy to drop straight into Instagram, LinkedIn, or a WhatsApp broadcast, since sharing this work is half the point.

If you're building or running a small business and any of this is useful, stick around — or get in touch if you want to talk about your own setup.
