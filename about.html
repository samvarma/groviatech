About

Founder-led. Hands-on with every build.

GroviaTech is a new studio — every automation and dashboard is designed and built directly by the founder, not handed off to a template.

S

Hi, I'm Sampath.

I started GroviaTech because I kept seeing the same pattern in small and growing businesses: good products, good service, but the systems behind the scenes — social media, customer follow-up, reporting — running on manual effort that doesn't scale.

GroviaTech exists to fix that specific gap: automating the repetitive work so business owners can spend their time on decisions only they can make, backed by dashboards that show what's actually happening in the business.

I write about the process on the blog — the tools, the mistakes, and what's actually working.
